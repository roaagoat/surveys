Q1 / 6 Double-barreled
"I am satisfied with my salary and my manager."
Problem
This bundles two completely separate things into one question. An employee happy with their pay but frustrated with their manager has no honest answer — whatever they pick, the data misrepresents them.
Better version
Split into two: "I am satisfied with my salary." and "I am satisfied with my manager."
